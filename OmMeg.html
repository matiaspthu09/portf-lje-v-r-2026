About Me

I grew up in Hellerudtoppen but I moved to Oppegård at the age of 3.

went to elementary school at Greverud skole and middle school Flåtestad skole but I moved to Oslo in 10th grade and started at Skøyenåsen skole.

My family is kind and supportive, I have a brother, a mom and dad and a dog.

Outside of school I like to play video games and watch shows and movies with my dad and my brother and I like to build Legos and read comics.

I would say I’m a kind person and would always help if someone needs help.

What’s important to me is being the best version of myself that I can and always be kind and help others. I have no goals in life I just take it as it comes and sees where it gets me. I don’t know what I want to work with.

The most interesting thing about me is that when I play a game I like I can reach the top ranks and be one of the best in my country if I really try (I have already in some games).

I use English every day to talk to my online friends and my teammates that I have in game, English is a big part of my life, and I would maybe say I know English better than Norwegian.

I would like to get better at IM, math and science. I learn English best by looking at how you use the words and using them in sentences, but I would say I can learn in any method.

My goal for English is to try and get at least a 5 and I’m always open for feedback.
